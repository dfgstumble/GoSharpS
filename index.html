List of the goals

Here are the the SDG's and the brief but important information related to them:

  1. No Poverty: End poverty in all it's forms everywhere
  2. Zero Hunger: End hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition and promote sustainable agriculture
  3. Good Health and Well Being: Promote well-being for all ages and ensure good health
  4. Quality education: Ensure inclusive and and equitable quality education for all and provide lifelong learning opportunities
  5. Gender Equality: Achieve gender equality and empower all woem and girls.
  6. Clean Water: This sdg strives to provide clean water
  7. Affordable and Clean Energy: This goal strives to provide reliable, modern and sustainable energy
  8. Decent Work and Economic Growth: This goal aims to provide decent work to all
  9. Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure: This aims to provide resilient and reliable infrastructure
  10. Reduce Inequalities: This aims to reduce inequalities among countries
  11. Sustainable Cities and Communities: Make cities and communities safe
  12. Responsible Consumption and Production: Thsi gaol aims for sustainable consumption
  13. Climate Action: This goal aims to combat climate change
  14. Life Below Water: This goal aims to save the life which is living underwater
  15. Life On Land: Same as Life Below Water, but for animals that live on land
  16. Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions: This aims to provide jsutice for everyone
  17. Partnership for the Goals: This goal aims for countries to be partners in completing the above goals
